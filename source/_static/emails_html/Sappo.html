Its SAPPOOOOOO TIME!!
Thames Valley Police went on a bear crocodile hunt this week after reports of the reptile been seen in flooded waters.   After a snappy journey to get there TVP found the reported crocodile to be a plastic toy.  On the other side of the pond a crocodile has been rescued after trying to get his morning coffee from Starbucks drive-thru.  Apparently, it was just another day on the job for the North Port Fire Rescue.  The crocodile has been safely relocated to a nearby pond. 
A rubber duck went swimming one day, over the hills* and far away...  The little guy might not have waddled over the Scottish Highlands but he did have quite a voyage having travelled 423 miles by sea from Dublin to Orkney which took him 18 years after being released as part of a World Record Duck Race in 2006.  Like this duck not all the ducks came swimming back to their owners as others also escaped into the sea and went on their own odysseys to Japan and Hawaii.  Hopefully they all had a quacking time!
Rubber ducks werent the only ones on the run after a zebra has made a dazzling** escape in Seattle this week.  Unlike the ducks it only took a few days for the zebras to be found as Shug the zebra and his friends had been seen hoofing around the foothills of Washington  after their escape from a trailer.  The local residents and animal control officers spent no time horsing around as they gathered the zebras together for their onward journey.
